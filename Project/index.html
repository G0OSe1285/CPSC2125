About the Author

Joanne Carney is a self-proclaimed book dragon who devours romance of all tropes. She has been writing since she was twelve and is an avid music lover of many genres. She currently resides in Georgia but is from Oregon. Joanne enjoys being out in nature when she's not writing. Her other hobbies include watching Anime and coloring.
